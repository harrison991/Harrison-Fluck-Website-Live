Frequently Asked Questions

What's your favorite adventure tour?

Each tour has been unique and special! The Girraween National Park trip was amazing for its natural beauty, while Vision Valley is challenging me in new ways.

How did you get into stage crew?

I've always been interested in the technical side of performances. I love working behind the scenes to make everything run smoothly!

Do you have any advice for outdoor adventures?

Always be prepared, work as a team, and embrace every challenge as a learning opportunity. The outdoors teaches you so much about yourself!

What's next for you?

I'm excited to continue my studies, keep participating in outdoor education programs, and maybe explore new activities like photography or coding!
